Questions

Frequently asked

How short should my phrase be?
Ideally 2–8 words. Think of it like a slogan, a name, or a short sentence. The shorter and punchier, the catchier the result. But we can work with longer phrases too — we'll help you refine it.
Can I use my own wording exactly?
Absolutely. You can choose to keep your exact words, or let us improve them for catchiness. It's your call — you stay in control.
Can I upload or record my voice?
Yes! You can record your voice directly in the configurator or describe the tone you want. This helps us match the energy and feel you're going for.
Can I describe my own style?
Of course. While we offer curated style options, you can always choose "Custom" and describe exactly what you have in mind. We love creative freedom.
Is this for both businesses and private people?
Yes. We offer two paths: Business jingles (from 97€) for brands, campaigns, and professional use — and Personal jingles (from 18€) for gifts, family moments, and fun.
What does "from 18€" and "from 97€" mean?
These are starting prices for the base package. The final price depends on your chosen add-ons (faster delivery, extra versions, etc.). The base package includes everything you need for a beautiful custom jingle.
Do I need musical knowledge?
Not at all. The entire experience is designed so anyone can shape a jingle with zero musical background. Just choose what feels right — we handle the rest.
Can I request revisions?
Yes. One revision round is included in every package. You can add additional revision rounds as an add-on if needed. We want you to love the result.
